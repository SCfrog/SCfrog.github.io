The Sticker Club

Stickers are a wonderful thing to have. They are decorative and useful. Sometimes, though, we just can't find the stickers that we want. If you want a specific picture, drawing, printout or image that you want on a sticker, you've come to the right place!


What we do

The Sticker Club makes custom stickers with the secret sticker recipe. We can make anything into a sticker, if it follows these simple conditions.

  • It must be school appropriate (unless you have special premission from an employee)
  • It CANNOT be scary, creepy or offending in any way. ( Unless you have special premission from an employee.)
  • If it is a gift, you have to say who it's for.
